Step 1 Wash the veggies
To begin with wash the veggies in some lukewarm water with some salt. Then rinse them with cold water, chop them and keep aside.

Step 2 Beat the yoghurt
Take a large bowl, add the yoghurt and beat it into a smooth paste. Next , add in the chopped veggies and sprinkle salt and pepper as per your taste.

Step 3 Garnish the salad and serve
Garnish the salad with fresh mint leaves, green chilli if you like and serve.
